# Welcome to Feyhaven

A narrative deck builder about moving to a new town.

## Artwork

You can see all the cards and their artwork [here](https://felixmo42.github.io/feyhaven/cards.html). All art is licensed under CC or is public domain. Credits are in the game or can be found in list.js.

Sources used to find artwork:
- https://www.wikiart.org/
- https://search.creativecommons.org/

**Do you want to contribute?** You can submit a PR or contact me directly! I don't have a ton of money to spend on this project, but I'm willing to pay ~**$75** for a nice art work that fits a card. Especially those that have bad images right now. I'll also put you in the credits and link to where you want.
